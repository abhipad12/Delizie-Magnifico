About Us

This restaurant has been a family owned business since 1985 and has grown tremendously ever since. We have been one of the oldest italian gluten-free bakeries and restaurants in the world. Our immpecable food creations do not lack any flavor or quality despite it all being gluten-free. From our famous bakery items such as the gluten-free chocolate mousse pie to our great restaurant dishes like the chicken marsala.

Our renowned award winning chefs from all around the world have come up with a classic Italian style menu with a ranging variety of deserts with a gluten-free take.
